Tribute to Black History

Honoring those who paved the way for change, justice, and equality.

Martin Luther King Jr.

Leader of the Civil Rights Movement, known for his message of non-violence and the famous "I Have a Dream" speech.

Harriet Tubman

Abolitionist and political activist who led hundreds of enslaved people to freedom via the Underground Railroad.

Malcolm X

Influential human rights activist who advocated for Black empowerment and resistance against racial oppression.

Maya Angelou

Poet, singer, memoirist, and civil rights activist, best known for her autobiography "I Know Why the Caged Bird Sings."
